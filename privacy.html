Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Welcome to GPSStamp Camera+. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

GPSStamp Camera+ is designed with a privacy-first approach. We operate without requiring user accounts or sign-ups. The types of data we interact with include:

  • Location Data: To provide core features like exact coordinates, altitude, headings, and maps, the App requires access to your device's GPS and location services. This data is used entirely on-device to geostamp your photos.
  • Camera & Photos: We require access to your device's camera to take photos and your photo library to save the geostamped images.
  • Environmental Data: To display live Air Quality Index (AQI), Weather, and Dust data, the App may send an anonymized location coordinate to a third-party weather API. No personal identifiers are attached to this request.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data accessed by the App exclusively to provide the App's stated functionality:

  • To overlay geographical coordinates, maps, and timestamps onto your photos.
  • To retrieve accurate local environmental and weather data for your geostamps.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

Because there is no sign-in or account system, all generated photos, videos, and associated metadata remain strictly on your local device or your personal iCloud storage (if enabled). We do not sell, rent, or trade your location data to advertisers or data brokers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may utilize third-party APIs (such as Apple Maps or Weather APIs) to fetch map imagery or live weather conditions. These requests are anonymous and only include the geographic coordinates necessary to return the requested data layer. By using the App, you also agree to the privacy practices of these essential third-party service providers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
